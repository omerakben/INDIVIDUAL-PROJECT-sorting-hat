Welcome to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry!

Greetings, young witch or wizard! As you step through the grand doors of Hogwarts, it's time for one of the most important moments of your journey - your house selection! Here, we bring the legendary Hogwarts Sorting Hat to life, just as it has done for centuries.

The Sorting Hat will place you into one of the four renowned Hogwarts houses:

  • Gryffindor, where bravery and courage shine.
  • Hufflepuff, where loyalty and hard work are celebrated.
  • Ravenclaw, where wisdom and knowledge guide the way.
  • Slytherin, where ambition and cunning lead to greatness.
