Welcome to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry!

Greetings, young witch or wizard! As you step through the grand doors of Hogwarts, it's time for one of the most important moments of your journey; your house sorting ceremony! Here, we bring the legendary Sorting Hat to life, just as it has done for centuries at Hogwarts.

The Sorting Hat will place you into one of the four noble Hogwarts houses:

  • Gryffindor, where dwell the brave at heart.
  • Hufflepuff, where loyalty, patience, and hard work are valued.
  • Ravenclaw, where those of wit and learning always find their kind.
  • Slytherin, where those with ambition and resourcefulness will thrive.
